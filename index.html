About bkeefi

bkeefi is a cutting-edge virtual reality (VR) platform that redefines the way sports fans experience live events. It offers a fully interactive, 360-degree viewing experience that brings the excitement and intensity of the stadium directly into the home. Fans can watch a wide range of live sports events in real-time, with the ability to customize viewing angles and access exclusive behind-the-scenes content.

Vision

To become the leading virtual sports Platform in the world, providing unparalleled experiences that inspire passion, community, and innovation within the sports industry.

Mission

To revolutionize sports engagement in Saudi Arabia by creating an immersive Virtual Reality Platform that connects fans to their chosen sports, athletes, and events, fostering a deeper connection to the Saudi sports ecosystem while driving technological innovation in alignment with Saudi Vision 2030.
